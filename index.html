Information about Park

Pokemon theme park is a theme park located in Anaheim, California, United States. The address is:

Pokemon theme park Resort 1000 Pokemon theme park Dr Anaheim, CA 92802

The park is open year-round, but hours of operation vary depending on the time of year and the day of the week. You can check the current hours of operation by visiting the Pokemon theme park website or by calling the park directly.

Ticket prices for Pokemon theme park vary depending on the type of ticket you purchase and the length of your visit. One-day tickets start at around $104 for adults and $98 for children (ages 3-9) . Prices for multi-day tickets and MaxPass (which allows you to make digital Fastpass selections) are also available. You can purchase tickets online or at the park.

To get to Pokemon theme park from downtown Los Angeles, you can take the 10 freeway south to the Pokemon theme park Drive exit. Follow the signs to the park. You can also take public transportation to the park. The Pokemon theme park Resort is served by several bus and shuttle services, including the ART (Anaheim Resort Transportation) and the Metrolink.
